# Maintenance Components

![Maintenance Logs](../../../assets/maintanancelogs.png)

This image shows the maintenance logs interface, tracking preventative and reactive maintenance events. Vehicles are automatically updated to "In Shop" status when maintenance is logged, preventing their dispatch until service is complete.

Manages maintenance and service logs for vehicles.

- **MaintenanceForm.tsx**: Form to log maintenance events.
- **MaintenanceLogs.tsx**: List and track maintenance history.

**Purpose:**

- Track preventative and reactive maintenance
- Automatically update vehicle status to "In Shop" when logged
- Exclude vehicles in maintenance from dispatcher selection
